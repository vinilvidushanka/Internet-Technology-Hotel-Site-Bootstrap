Anantara Peace Haven Tangalle Resort, Sri Lanka

Anantara Peace Haven lies on the southern tip of the island, on a rocky outcrop overlooking the Indian Ocean. Tangalle, Sri Lanka is known for its wide sandy beaches, and our hotel boasts an enviable stretch of coastline.

Getting Here

Anantara Peace Haven Tangalle Resort is located 220 km from Bandaranaike International Airport, 190 km from the capital, Colombo, and 70 km from Mattala International Airport.
